## About Synthonyx

Synthonyx is a European technology company dedicated to building sovereign infrastructure solutions for organizations that require control over their data and technology stack.

### Our Mission

To provide enterprise-grade, sovereign technology infrastructure that enables European organizations to operate independently of non-EU technology providers while maintaining the highest standards of security, compliance, and performance.

### Our Values

- **Sovereignty**: Data and technology independence for European organizations
- **Security**: Zero-trust architecture and military-grade protection
- **Transparency**: Open communication about our practices and policies
- **Excellence**: Enterprise-grade reliability and performance

### Leadership

Our leadership team brings decades of experience in enterprise technology, cybersecurity, and European regulatory compliance.
