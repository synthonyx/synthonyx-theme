## Sovereign Cloud Platform

Sovereign is our flagship infrastructure platform designed for organizations that require absolute control over their data within European borders.

### Key Features

- **European Data Residency**: All data stored and processed exclusively within EU borders
- **GDPR Compliance**: Built-in compliance tooling and audit trails
- **Zero-Trust Architecture**: Military-grade encryption for all data in transit and at rest
- **High Availability**: 99.99% uptime SLA with geographic redundancy
- **API-First Design**: Full REST and GraphQL APIs for automation

### Use Cases

- Government and public sector
- Financial services
- Healthcare organizations
- Legal and compliance-sensitive industries
