## BSOS Platform

BSOS (Business Operating System) provides enterprise-grade operating systems and solutions for organizations undergoing digital transformation.

### Key Features

- **Hardened Security**: Pre-configured security policies and compliance frameworks
- **Enterprise Integration**: Native support for enterprise directories and identity providers
- **Lifecycle Management**: Automated updates, patching, and configuration management
- **Multi-Cloud Ready**: Deploy on-premises, private cloud, or sovereign infrastructure

### Modules

- **BSOS Core**: Base operating system with enterprise tooling
- **BSOS Secure**: Additional hardening for compliance-sensitive workloads
- **BSOS Cloud**: Optimized for cloud-native deployments

### Industries

- Financial services and banking
- Healthcare and life sciences
- Manufacturing and industrial
- Government and defense
