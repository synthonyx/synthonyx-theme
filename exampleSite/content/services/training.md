## Training Programs

Develop the skills your team needs to design, deploy, and operate sovereign infrastructure effectively.

### Certification Tracks

We offer comprehensive certification programs for technology professionals:

- **Sovereign Cloud Architect**: Master the design and deployment of sovereign cloud infrastructure
- **BSOS Administrator**: Learn to install, configure, and manage BSOS environments
- **DORA Compliance Specialist**: Deep expertise in Digital Operational Resilience Act requirements
- **Security Operations**: Advanced security monitoring and incident response

### Delivery Formats

Choose the format that works best for your team:

- **On-Site Workshops**: Intensive hands-on training at your location
- **Virtual Instructor-Led**: Live online training with interactive labs
- **Self-Paced Online**: Flexible learning with video lectures and exercises
- **Hands-On Labs**: Practice in sandboxed sovereign environments

### Custom Programs

We develop custom training curricula tailored to your organization's specific needs, technology stack, and compliance requirements.

### Certification Benefits

- Recognized across European technology organizations
- Demonstrates expertise in sovereign infrastructure
- Includes hands-on practical assessments
- Ongoing access to updated materials

### Schedule

Public training sessions are offered monthly. Private sessions can be scheduled at your convenience.
