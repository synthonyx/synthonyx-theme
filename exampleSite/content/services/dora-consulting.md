## DORA Compliance Services

Expert guidance for financial institutions preparing for Digital Operational Resilience Act (DORA) regulation.

### Our Approach

We provide comprehensive DORA compliance services tailored to your organization's specific needs and current maturity level.

- **Gap Analysis**: Thorough assessment of your current ICT risk management practices against DORA requirements
- **Policy Development**: Creation of required policies, procedures, and documentation
- **Framework Implementation**: Hands-on implementation of ICT risk management frameworks
- **Third-Party Risk**: Assessment and management of third-party ICT service provider risks
- **Incident Reporting**: Establishing procedures for ICT incident reporting to competent authorities

### Deliverables

- Comprehensive DORA compliance roadmap with prioritized actions
- Documentation templates and policy frameworks
- Staff training and awareness programs
- Ongoing compliance monitoring and support

### Timeline

DORA becomes fully applicable in January 2025. We can help you achieve compliance before the deadline.
